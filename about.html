About

UGUR OZDEMIR

In 2017, exact 500 years after my far far
grandfather Ozdemir Pasa brought the Yemen
and Ethiopian coffee beans to Istanbul, I started
to think about designing professional Turkish
coffee machine for commercial use.

After a wide research I have noticed that there
was no professional Turkish coffee machine
while everyday half of the world is enjoying
Turkish coffee.

Without any experience in developing
machines, not knowing how a coffee machine
and coffee grinder works, what cnc and
diecasting is, how electronics work, we started
to design the first professional Turkish coffee
machine in the history.

After many testings with sensors and heaters,
several prototypes, building up relations with
Chinese partners, talkings with Turkish and
Espresso barista's, we now have reached a level
on which we proudly can present the worlds first
professional Turkish coffee machine ever made.
